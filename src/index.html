How to play

  1. Click on 'New Game' to create a new Dai Di Game where you can invite your friends by selecting their names from the drop down select inputs
  2. Click on 'See Invites' to see the games that you have started / are invited to
  3. Once all players have joined the game, the game will start and the players will be dealt their hand of cards

Game Rules

The aim of the game is to be the first to get rid of all the cards on hand

The player with 3 of diamonds will start the first round, which will continue until 3 players consecutively skipped their turn. When that happens, the round ends and the last player who player their hand gets to begin a new round

For within each round, players are only allowed to play a hand that is of the same combination and has a higher value.

Card Combinations allowed in the game are:

  • Singles
  • Doubles
  • Triples
  • Any 5-card hand
